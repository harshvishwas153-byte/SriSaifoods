Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about scanning, claiming, and receiving your cashback.

How do I claim cashback?
Scan the QR code inside your Sri Sai packet, enter your UPI ID when prompted, and submit. Your cashback is sent directly to that UPI ID after verification.
Where is the QR located?
The reward QR code is printed on a small insert or directly on the inner side of eligible Sri Sai packets. Not every product or pack currently carries a QR.
Can I claim twice?
No. Each QR code can only be redeemed once. Once a code has been successfully claimed, it cannot be used again.
How long does cashback take?
Once your QR and UPI ID are verified, cashback is typically processed within a few business days.
