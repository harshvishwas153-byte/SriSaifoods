Our Legacy

State-of-the-Art Processing Facility

Sri Sai's Snacks has been pioneering food processing. By merging traditional Indian seasonings with modern twin-screw cooking extrusion systems, we produce snacks with high puff ratios and long-lasting freshness.

We source top-grade potatoes, corn grits, wheat flours, and pure oil, running all products through automatic optical sorters and metal-screening lines to guarantee safety.

1. Ingredient Mixing & Conditioning
Potato starch, grains, and spice mixes are blended uniformly under strict temperature levels.
2. Twin-Screw Extrusion Shaping
The cooked paste is forced through custom shaped precision dies (wheels, rings, pipes).
3. Gentle Drying & Moisture Lock
Multi-pass warm air dryers dehydrate the pellets to under 9% moisture for stable shelf-life.
4. Automatic Packet Packaging
Fried snacks are seasoned, weighed, and sealed in foil packets (Rs.1, Rs.2, Rs.5) under nitrogen flush.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers for distributor operations, shelf-life, and private labeling supply.

What is the shelf life of the packed snacks?
Our fully seasoned and packed snack packets have a shelf life of 6-8 months, thanks to moisture-barrier metallic foil packaging and nitrogen gas flushing. Raw pellets have a shelf life of 12 months.
Do you customize shapes and seasonings?
Yes! We can engineer custom shapes using specialized extrusion dies, and customize spices (chili, tomato, onion, or custom tang levels) to meet your exact distribution profiles.
What is the wholesale minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
For standard brands (Sri Sai's / Madan Ji's), the MOQ is 500 KG. For customized seasonings or shape runs, the MOQ is 1,500 KG.
How do you manage freight transport?
We arrange direct road cargo loaders, tempos, and multi-ton trucks across Gujarat, Maharashtra, and other regions, as well as full-container-loads (FCL) for exports from major ports (like Mundra or Nhava Sheva).
